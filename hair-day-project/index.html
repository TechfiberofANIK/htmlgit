Meet Pria & Garry

At Hair Day, we believe that hair care should be a transformative experience that caters to both your style and your spirit. Founded by the powerhouse duo of Pria, our master color specialist, and Garry, our resident styling expert, we have built a space that transcends the traditional salon model to become a true sanctuary. In an industry that can often feel rushed, we prioritize a warm, welcoming environment where you can truly unwind. Our collective of seven distinct artists works in harmony to provide a unique blend of technical precision and wild creativity. Whether you are seeking a complete color overhaul, a precision cut, or a bold new aesthetic, our team treats every appointment as a collaborative masterpiece. We are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of traditional hair design while ensuring every guest feels seen, heard, and completely at home.
